How do I get Permissions?

Make a Blog post

All of our blog stuff is handled by another website named Blogger. To make a blog post, your google account must be added as an "Author" by an Admin of the blog. 

To see if you have permission to post on the blog, click the following link and sign into your Google account.

Blogger Posts Page

From there, if you have permission, it's as simple as hitting "New Post"

Add an Author to the Blog

To add another author to the blog, you must be an "Admin" on the blog page.

Click the following link. If you have permissions, scroll down and then hit "Add Author" to add in more authors via their Google account.

Blogger Basic Admin Settings

Link other people to our Blog

You can link other people to our blog in three ways:

  • www.blog.team5599.com
  • www.team5599.blogspot.com
  • www.team5599.com --> Blog & Media --> Our Blog
  • A google search of "Team 5599 Blog"

Google Calendars

The Team Calendar is just a Google Calendar.

Your Google acount must be added in to be able to make changes, add events, and add other users to the calendar.

On your mobile device or on the google calendars website, you can edit the calendar as you would your normal Google Calendar.

Click the link below and sign in with your Google account to see if you can access the Calendar settings. If not, someone has to add you in.
